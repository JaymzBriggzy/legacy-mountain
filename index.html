A Whole New Adventure

Discover what Legacy Mountain has to offer! Beautiful and open space enjoy to limitless activities that everyone can enjoy!

Scenic Drives
Drive along the Wasatch Plateau for over 70 miles of scenic views of the Manit-La Sal National Forest! With elevations over 10,000 ft. you are bound to discover Utah's favorite backroad!
Fishing
Choose from over 7 nearby lakes or reservoirs for prime and relaxing fishing! Plus with over 12 additional places to fish within less than a 30 minute drive, you'll never run out of new places to fish!
Horseback Riding
Enjoy miles and miles of horseback riding trails. Or take your horses along the Skyline Drive to see breathtaking views of the beautiful Sanpete Valley.
Off-Road Adventure
Enjoy one of the largest networks of ATV and off roading trails in the State of Utah connecting you to nature in a whole new way while discovering all the different lakes in the Manit-La Sal Forest.
Biking
See what all the locals are talking about by discovering the thrill and the cool breeze as you take your bike on the high adventure bike trails. There's a trail for all levels of mountain bikers!
Exploration
Come across wildlife from squirrels, birds, deer and elk as you explore the beautiful mountain, canyons, and hiking trails! What gems will you discover?
